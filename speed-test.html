Tips to Improve Your Typing Speed

  • Practice regularly - even 10-15 minutes daily can make a difference
  • Maintain proper posture with your feet flat on the floor
  • Keep your wrists elevated and fingers curved
  • Learn touch typing to avoid looking at the keyboard
  • Start slow and focus on accuracy first, speed will follow
  • Take breaks to avoid strain and fatigue
